## Contributing Guidelines

Last updated: January 10, 2026

We welcome community contributions! Thank you for your interest in improving SQLMind Studio.

All contributors are expected to follow our
[Code of Conduct](https://github.com/sqlmind-studio/sqlmind-studio/blob/master/code_of_conduct.md).

---

### Opening Issues

We use issue templates for bug reports, feature requests, and questions. Please follow the instructions in the templates.

When opening an issue, include:
- Your operating system
- Database type and version
- SQLMind Studio version
- Clear steps to reproduce the issue (for example, relevant SQL statements)

---

### Pull Requests

We recommend opening an issue before starting work on a significant change to avoid duplicate effort and to discuss design approaches.
